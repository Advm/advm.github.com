About Me

I am a first-generation graduate from Hamilton College with a B.A. in Computer Science and Mathematics and a minor in Education. I have a strong interest in software development with a focus on equal access to technological education. My passion is to aid in making technology as accessible to the most marginalized people in The United States through development, opportunity, and community involvement.
